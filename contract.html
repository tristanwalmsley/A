The Bestie Contract
Third Annual Renewal — Birthday Edition
Binding Across: England · The Netherlands · The North Sea

This agreement, entered into voluntarily and enthusiastically, between one Tristan (hereinafter "the Giver of Thoughtful Parcels") and one Ann (hereinafter "the Bestie"), constitutes a binding, renewable, and entirely sincere declaration of best friendship.

I. Both parties acknowledge that this friendship is exceptionally rare and should be treated as such. Most people go their whole lives without finding this. We found it. That's enormous.
II. The Bestie agrees to open all gifts in order as directed by this site, without skipping ahead, peeking at the bottom, or going rogue. The Giver has arranged things very deliberately.
III. Both parties are bound by the Plushie Accord (see Gift #8): the little plushie shall make the journey between our homes twice yearly, indefinitely, and be treated with the care befitting a small ambassador of our friendship.
IV. The Bestie is legally obligated to eat at least one biscuit on this call, in front of the Giver. No exceptions. No deferrals. No "I'll have it later."
V. Both parties agree that nothing productive shall be achieved on this call. This is not a criticism. This is the highest compliment.
VI. The Giver reserves the right to feel very smug about the decorated box, this website, and the Heinz Beans can. The Bestie agrees that this smugness is entirely warranted.
VII. Both parties hereby renew all previous bestie obligations and introduce a new clause: the Dutch Language Clause. The Giver has been learning Dutch for four months. This is objectively adorable and the Bestie agrees to acknowledge this accordingly.

  • Remain the kind of friends who celebrate ordinary Tuesdays
  • Exchange parcels across the North Sea at every suitable occasion
  • Always pick up where we left off, without any weirdness
  • Never take for granted how genuinely good this is
