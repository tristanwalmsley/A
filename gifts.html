How this works ✦

  1. Each stop gives you a clue about where to find the actual gift — not what it is.
  2. Go hunt for it for real, based on the clue.
  3. Once you're holding it, tap "I've got it — show me" to reveal what it was.
  4. Some stops unlock a new page on the site instead of a gift — same idea, just tap to open it.
  5. A few gifts come as a bundle and unlock all together, so you're not waiting between them.
