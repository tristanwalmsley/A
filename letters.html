Hi Bestie, it is currently 10th April 2026 while I write this letter, excuse the poor handwriting, I studied 10/11 hours today, it's 00:34 AM, i'm sleepy.

The past few nights, while I've been up late, I've been thinking about you and our relationship, and I've thought about this many times before. I assume you'll recieve this for your birthday this year, and for me birthdays are very vulnerable and ig for a moment of reflection, not just my birthday, but also those of who I care for (you :p). I feel like I never truly can tell or show you JUST how much I appreciate you, because nothing I can do or say does. I like to take these times of year to reflect on that, usually in cards, but I feel the need for more, hence this letter, which I may add to before it's sent (sorry if very long).

So yeah, like truly, you are the dream friend anyone could ask for and someone I never thought I'd find, yet you came at such a perfect time. You found me at the start of college, just after my traumatic high school, when my parents truly started to be a pure mental attack and drain, and lots of confusion about life and myself. When you met me you saw the reserve, emotion felt like something of weakness, and vulnerability I had suppressed, and I'd never had a true friend. These are effects I still live with, and I'm still reserved, not too confident, shy, emotion hard, and more. You (and for a short time, Wren) really helped me open up and actually learn who I even was, and while the 3 years to follow were the toughtest years I think i'll ever face, and with my mental health taking constant huge blows, you stuck around, you dealt with me, and without even knowing, you helped me. When I moved to uni my first set of problems began to heal, my reset, learning about my adhd, add, ocd, sexuality, and actually making IRL friends, you continued to stay and support me. And then to uni year 2, with me being able to open up more and continue to try and learn about myself. Along all this time you remained the one constant in my life who was always there and always a joy. I genuinely can't describe my happiness abt our calls, often I'll be smilling and grinning after calls, sat in excitement all day about our calls, and actually a new common issue, often my jaw hurts on call from smiling more than usual. Sometimes I'll be sat in bed at night, or in a random lecture, or on a train and something triggers a memory between us, and I'll get the hugest smile in my face or start laughing instintively and in public have to stop myself. Or we will be messaging and I just get so happy. Not to get really sad after that, but genuinely it's something that keeps me going, last summer (Jul - Oct 2025), and recent (Dec 2026 - Apr 2026) has been the worst my mental state has ever been in, sleepless nights, barely eating, not being able to do stuff, quite sad, or even thoughts about it all (that really are vivid and not pleasent [and dw, I know I won't act on the violent ones, they are just there however]). Throughout that, you one of the primary things that keep me going. It's quite a tough time for me now, and I'm not sure when it'll get better, I don't think it will soon, I have good days and very, very bad ones, but I know and hope it will eventually, when I'm ready to do something more, But again, throughout it, without even knowing how bad it may be at times, or how many times I've cried that day before I call, you've been there for me, and everyday, I somehow feel we become even better friends and besties. The day I get to meet you Irl, I think it'll heal something in me, before I then die of happiness.

I wrote a lot more than I thought I would, I just wrote without thinking and from the heart, so it may not be too coherent. I'll provide a digital version later if too unreadable in places. Again, I may also add too it, maybe another 2AM, and Idk if even I will reread this before I send it.

Thanks Bestie Ann.
