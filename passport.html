About the Plushie Tradition

This little plushie is the start of something that I hope will go on for a very long time. It lives at Ann's place for six months, then comes home to Tristan for six months — back and forth, crossing the sea twice a year, accumulating stamps in this passport.

Every journey it makes is a tiny physical reminder that we're still very much in each other's lives, even when we're many miles apart.
